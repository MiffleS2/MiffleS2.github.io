Project Design

Project Overview:

  • Application Purpose and Description:
    The purpose of this application is to build an interactive art gallery for a friend of mine who would like to display or potentially even sell some of his artwork.
  • Intended Users:
    The intended users of this website would be potential buyers for artwork, or people just interested in seeing my friends portfolio of work.
  • Overview of Content:
    The website will have a few different possible navigation pathways. A landing page, photo gallery, contact information, location information, and an about section.
